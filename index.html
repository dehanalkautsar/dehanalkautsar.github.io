I am a Research Engineer in MBZUAI focusing on Multilinguality & Dialogue in NLP. My work explores on how LLM can be more inclusive towards underrepresented languages. I enjoy combining rigorous methods with practical applications that make a real-world impact.

On this page you’ll find a brief overview of my education, selected publications, recent news, and how to get in touch.
